This year's MTV Movie and TV Awards will honor some old favorites and new delights. Blockbuster film "Top Gun: Maverick" joins TV shows "Stranger Things," "The Last of Us," "The White Lotus" and "Wednesday" with the most nominations among the scripted content. "Jersey Shore Family Vacation," "RuPaul's Drag Race," "The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills" and "Vanderpump Rules" lead nominations in the unscripted categories. Hosted by Drew Barrymore, the awards show will air live from th Barker Hangar Santa Monica, California on Sunday, May 7, starting at 8 p.m.ET/PT.
