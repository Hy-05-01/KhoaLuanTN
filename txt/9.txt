Nimi Princewill is a Nigerian journalist famed for exposing government corruption in his home country. Before joining CNN in 2021, Nimi worked as a reporter at Peoples Gazette, a Nigerian online newspaper notable for its investigative journalism. Nimi reported exclusively on a number of high-profile stories, including the disbandment of the brutal police unit known as SARS, hours before the official announcement. Follow him on Twitter @princewill_nimi.
