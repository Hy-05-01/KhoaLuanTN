Anna Cooban is a global markets writer for CNN Business. She leads international coverage of markets news out of London, and reports on shifts in business and the global economy. Since joining CNN in 2021, Cooban has reported on some of the biggest stories emerging from Russia's invasion of Ukraine, notably, the turmoil in Europe's energy market and impact of soaring inflation on businesses, households and investor behavior. Before CNN, Cooban worked as a policy maker for the Mayor of London's post-pandemic economic recovery program. Prior to that, she spent three years as a management consultant. Cooban is a graduate of Oxford University.
