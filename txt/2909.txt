Jessica Reyes is Vice President of Product Management for video and audio. In her role, she oversees product strategy and development for all of CNN Digital's video, podcast and audio offerings. Previously, she served a Senior Director overseeing CNN's streaming product strategy. Prior to that role, she served as Director, developing personnel strategy that supports the overall business goals of the company. Prior to joining CNN, Reyes spent three years at Televisa as the Director of Product and Project Management. There she directed the management of Blim, an OTT service available in Latin America. Additionally she developed the product and technology roadmap, managing the entire product line lifecycle. Before Televisa, Reyes also held positions with Univision, CBS Sports, and WPBT2. Reyes received a Bachelors of Science in Communications from Florida International University. Originally a native of Miami, Reyes now lives in in Atlanta.
