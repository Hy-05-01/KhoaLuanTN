Veronica Stracqualursi is a Campaign Producer for CNN covering the 2024 presidential election. Stracqualursi covered the 2022 midterm elections from Wisconsin, where she reported on the state's US Senate and gubernatorial races. She previously was a breaking news writer for CNN Politics and wrote extensively about the White House, Congress, federal agencies, the issue of abortion, the Equal Rights Amendment and gun legislation. Prior to joining CNN, Stracqualursi was a production assistant with ABC News Digital. She covered the 2016 election and the Trump administration and helped author "The Note," ABC News' political weekday morning newsletter. She's a proud member of the Asian American Journalists Association. Stracqualursi grew up in Massachusetts and graduated from the University of Massachusetts Amherst.
