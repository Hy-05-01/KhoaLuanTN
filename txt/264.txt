Justice Clarence Thomas and his wife, conservative activist Ginni Thomas, have gone on several luxury trips involving travel subsidized by and stays at properties owned by GOP megadonor Harlan Crow, according to a new bombshell ProPublica report. CNN's Ariane de Vogue reports. Thomas said that the trips he and his wife took with Crow were the "sort of personal hospitality from close personal friends" that he was advised did not require disclosure.
